What're my essential coffee products?

Espresso Machine

Of course, it goes with out saying, an espresso machine is essential for making barista style coffees at home! You can get a range of machines, all differing in prices - the higher end ones are very expensive! I have the Sage Duo Temp Pro!

Coffee Scales

It is crutical to use a pair of scales made for coffee when making espresso, this ensures the extraction time and ratio is correct. For example, to make a double shot, you would weight out 17-18g of coffee beans, and extract 34-36g of liquid (espresso).

Coffee Grinder

In order to get the freshest possible coffee, being able to grind your own coffee beans makes all of the difference! You can get electric or manual grinders - both are awesome! I have the Sage Dose Control grinder.

Distributor & WDT tool

Using the WDT tool helps break up the ground coffee so there is no clumps which would result in an un-even extraction. After using the WDT tool, I use the distributor to even out the coffee grounds (again, for an even extraction)!

Tamp

A tamp is a important piece of equipemnt for making espresso, applying force to porterfilter ensures the coffee is compressed and allows an even flow of water to come through.
